Section 8 — Key Insights
📊 Meta Drives 2.4× More Volume

Meta generates 304 leads vs 128 from the website. In absolute numbers: 40 relevant from Meta vs 17 from Website — purely due to volume advantage.

✅ Relevancy Rate Nearly Identical

Meta = 41.2%, Website = 42.5% — less than 2% gap. Both sources produce the same quality. The problem is not ad targeting.

🔴 Website Unreachable Rate = 42%

54 of 128 website leads (42%) are unreachable vs only 5.3% for Meta. Website forms may collect wrong numbers. Meta pre-fills phone via Facebook — far more reliable.

⚠ 207 Leads Still Unqualified

Only 97 Meta and 40 Website leads assessed. The final relevancy split will shift as the team qualifies remaining leads through the rest of May.

🏢 Top Products — Relevant Leads

Meta: Workshop & Events (28), Offices (9). Website: Offices (11), Workshop (3), Meeting Rooms (3). Meta stronger for events; Website leans office.

📍 Downtown Dominates Both Sources

75% of both Meta and Website leads target Downtown – Tahrir. Mall of Arabia West = ~25%. Location split is consistent across both channels.
